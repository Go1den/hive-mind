How to play:

Score points by making words using the tiles provided to you.
You can use the same tile more than once, but you must always use the center tile in every word.
Words must be between 4 and 17 letters.
Score 1 point for 4 letter words, and n points for n > 4 letter words.
Score an extra 7 points if your word uses every letter at least once.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Type any letter to add it to the word.
Space Bar - Shuffles the tiles in your tray.
Backspace - Removes the last typed letter.
Enter - Submits a guess, or types the last submitted guess automatically.
